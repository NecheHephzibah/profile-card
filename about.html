My Story

I’ve always been drawn to how technology can make life simpler, and how design can make it beautiful. My journey into web development started with curiosity. I had that desire to not just use digital tools, but to create them. Every new concept I learn feels like unlocking a small piece of magic, and each project teaches me something new about how creativity and logic can coexist.

My Goals

My biggest goal is to become a professional developer, one who writes clean, efficient, and meaningful code. I want to build products that make people’s lives better and grow into a developer who’s both technically skilled and creatively confident. I hope to get to that point where I create that solution that resonates with majority of the world's population.

Areas I’m Working On

I sometimes doubt how quickly I’m improving compared to others. There are moments I hesitate before taking on challenging projects, I worry that I might not get everything right the first time. But I’ve learned that confidence grows with a lot of practice and I’m definitely learning to trust the process more.
